    To Be Continued ...

    We hope you enjoyed the first issue of Castle Marrach: Awakenings. It's the first part of a planned arc of six issues, and we hope to be able to offer those other five issues in the future.

    In Part 2 of 6, "The Watch", Victor continues to try and make his way in the Castle by joining one of the Castle's martial organizations. Meanwhile the awakenings continue and Queen Vivienne begins preparations for a mysterious quest.
