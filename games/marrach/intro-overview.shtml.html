Overview

You awaken within the Castle Marrach, dressed in linen wrappings and bereft of memory.

Within the cold, stone walls of this Castle you will meet many others whose only memory is of this place. It is a Castle split in twain, a home to ancient peoples, a place of new opportunity.

There is a society within Castle Marrach, divided between the Inner Bailey, the Outer Bailey, and those realms below, and you must find your place within it. Duelists, the Poets' Brotherhood, the Winter Watch, and the Remembers are just a few of the guilds within the Outer Bailey — groups that you may join to discover your own niche within this new world.

Mystery, romance, chivalry, fantasy, and intrigue combine in the story of Castle Marrach.
