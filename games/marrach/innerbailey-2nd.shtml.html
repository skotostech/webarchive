Second Floor

Collegium: The Royal Collegium's Office. The three Headmasters and Dean may be found here.

Inner Guest Suite: The middle floor of the three sets of luxurious suites allocated to those lucky enough to live within the walls of the Inner Bailey.

Ladies' Hall: Decorated by the Handmaid Isaria, this tasteful room is designed for relaxation and womanly pursuits.

Library: The upper floor and reading room of the Royal Library. The reading room is a cozy place to sit and chat with your peers.

Lords' Rooms: Lavishly furnished suites of rooms allocated to the peerage and their households.
