Andrius, tired from his days exploits, was pale white and extremely dirty. He pictured his friend, from the moment they met in the Academy. Sparring together, playing jokes on the younger members of the Legio in later years, but mainly walking around the streets each day, carefree. He wondered what it would be like if they had simply gone to lunch that day, no battle, no Cinerans, no death.
