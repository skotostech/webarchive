Lost to the Dawn

By Merse


I took you in and sheltered you from the elements.
My cloak I gave to keep the cold from you.

My sword drawn in your defense,
blood shed by my hands to keep harm from you.

Wounds great and small I endured knowing you were at my side.
In my most desperate hour I turn to you to find you gone

Lost like a shadow against the dawn

I sit alone and cold in this dank hold
without clothes or blade

Shivering in the dampness
I finally surrender to sleep my last thought of you
