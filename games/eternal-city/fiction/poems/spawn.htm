The Raven Spawn

By Appolus

The rage inside a heart of evil
muffling the good within
the eyes as red as fresh spilled blood
the Raven spawn awakens

A prick of crimson in the night
his eyes are brightly burning
the hatred flowing in his veins
the Raven spawn awakens

An evil deed he plans to do
a heinous crime he plots
his machinations tainted
the Raven spawn awakens

He shambles 'mongst the rustling leaves
his hands are bent to claws
a hideous form of twisted sinew
the Raven spawn awakens

A devil now no longer human
his drives no longer sane
the bloodlust is his only crave
the Raven spawn awakens

Atop the corpse of men he feeds
his teeth are stained with blood
the only way to free his torment
the Raven spawn awakens

He lurks within the hearts of men
just waiting to be released
whenever man is filled with anger
the Raven spawn awakens
