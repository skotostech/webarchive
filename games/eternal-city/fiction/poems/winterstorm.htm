Winter Storm

By Kaylarae

Winter Storm

Rain shrieks,
Ice wind howls,
Lightning streaks,
Thunder growls,

Tortured skies,
In anquish, weep -
Flooding their cries,
On rivers deep

Daggers of grass,
Frost ice blades,
Shattered, they pass,
To beaten glades

Pathways misted,
In foggy defiance,
Bare limbs twisted,
From bearded giants,

Impotent, lurching,
Largest and least,
Shelter searching,
Man and beast.
