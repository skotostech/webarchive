The Stars

By Othelia

As I lay in the alley,beneath the sky,
I gazed above me,into the nigh.
I watched the planets gently collide,
And remembered how you,with me,would abide.

I closed my eyes,blocked night from view,
But instead of the darkness all i saw was you.
With a gentle kindness,your eyes shone,
My one true friend,I cant believe you are gone.

I opened my eyes to see a falling star,
And i like to believe that is where you are;
Resting in heaven,without a care,
And i hope that ,someday,I shall meet with you there.

(Dedicated to my greatly missed friend Necros;May Ereal bless your spirit.)
