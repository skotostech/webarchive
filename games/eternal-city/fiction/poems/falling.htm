falling into you

By Val

i come upon what time forgot
sitting to reflect the lot
what remains of this hellbent wreck
falling into deeper wells

what happened that makes people like me
so stiff and rigid, no longer free
to dwell what once was mine to dwell
not worrying about the bottomless well
falling deeper into thee

one day i will understand
one day i will comprehend
why i fall deeper into thee
be it love or a weaker force
or what lies in between
