Sparing

By Taliseman

Common,
You might have better armor,
And better success,
But I have spirit,
For me that works the best!

In practice or not,
I do not care,
I take my wounds in pride,
And return your strikes side by side.

Healers,
They are my best friends,
Small slashes and gashes,
They all mend.

I don't care how experianced you are,
I will join you in a friendly spar,
If just friendly is what you seek, for if you attack me without any need,
And wound me for no reason, I will track you down,
And kill you with my Glad.
