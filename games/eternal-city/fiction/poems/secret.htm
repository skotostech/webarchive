The Secret

By Jominey

How do I let you know,
just how deeply I feel?
To let you know,
t'was my heart you did steal.
Would it take a month? a year?
or a decade or two?
Nay, not even my lifetime,
or eternity would do.
I've hear the eyes,
are the window to the sould.
If you looked into mine,
then would you know?
