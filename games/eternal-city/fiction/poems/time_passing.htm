Time's passing

By Bjergar


I lost my world today, my reality,
my mate - I thought my soul mate,
'Soul mate?' I ask now,
for can one so young know love.

Alone in this shattered world,
thinking about the past,
and the pain reels not only in the heart,
but also in the feet, legs, and up the body.

Alone, staggering along,
muscles aching, head hurting,
tears streaming endlessly,
in this world of mine.

A step forward, sighing,
and stalling, but another step,
drying tears, oh let them dry,
and a third step.

Think positive, and happy,
move on, move on,
look forward, not past,
and think of possibilities..
