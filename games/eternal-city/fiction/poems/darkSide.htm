The Dark Side

By Lilith



Day is gone, Night begins,
Darkness consumes me
The light fades away.
Nothing is left.
Not one ray of hope.
I'm lost in the blackness.
Yet not alone.
A dark figure appears.
Leading me deeper and deeper,
To the point of no return.

Is this the end?
Is this where it stops?
One step, Two step,
I walk on and on.
Following closely.
Not knowing why.
Not knowing where.
I'm lead blindly down the squishy path.

No sign of life.
Only the figure.
Three step, Four step.
Unaware of my surroundings.
I'm a slave to the dark side.
