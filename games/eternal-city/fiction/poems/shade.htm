Shade

By Petra

You look like a Courtesan,
Sing like an Angel,
Fight like a Demon,
With the smile of a Child.

A Lady who flutters,
While flicking her whip,
A sweet little armful,
Who would give the Gods lip.

Helping the newbies
With an able assist,
Ko! spoken to Andrel
Or Quint gets you kissed.
