Love Letters

By Moonlite

This is a collection of love letters that I recieved when I was 15.
-Moonlite A. Crashton


To my dearest Moonlite,
My father has become a constable and has found out that your father is a thief. I am to stay away from you, and never look or speak to you again. I am sickened by the way my father thinks of you, only because of the way your father is. I’m so sorry. Tonight at midnight, meet me in the last hall of the Stone Toga Inn. I have a gift for you.
I love you,
Alex

Dear Moonlite,
I am thinking about running away from home and renting a room in the Stone Toga Inn. My mother went to Monlon to shop and has never returned. Since then my father has treated me like a slave and it is a hideous feeling. Do not tell anyone about me running away, I would be killed. Please visit me in my new room every 3 days, I will tell you what room it is soon.
Love,
Alex

Dearest Moonlite,
I have run away and I am in my new room. It is the room at the very end of the hall in the Stone Toga Inn. Please visit me tonight, I must speak with you. I know the Stone Toga Inn is far from the Safelands, but it is a safe place to hide.
Your Love,
Alex

To my Dear one,
Moonlite, my father has moved away to Monlon, looking for my mother. He does not care about me; he just left a note on the doorstep that he has left me. Oh yes, I suggest that you keep these notes in a safe place, like your keep sake chest.
I miss you,
Alex

Hey Alex,
Would you stop being so mushy on my sister? I mean, geez! Gie her a break! Her poor heart is gonna explode for heavens sake!
Moonlite’s brother,
Herc N. Crashton

 To my dearest Alex,
My brother Herc and Starhead have found your love notes and are making fun of me. What shall I do? I have found a key for my keep sake chest and I am hiding it under my bed. At least my sister Sunshine has not found out. That would be terrible.
I love you so much,
Moonlite A. Crashton

 Dearest Moonlite,
Your brother Herc has sent me a note telling me not to be so mushy on you. I hope you are okay with this. I have left the Stone Toga Inn, for I have spent my last sen. I have no money left to pay rent. Now I am just a wondering lost soul.
I love you every day,
Alex

Dear Moonlite,
I believe this will be my last note to you, for I am hiding from the Solders. They are looking for me because I saw the plan. Moonlite, no matter what happens to me I will love you and never stop. Do not forget me.
I love you,
Alex

Dear Moonlite,
Today I was in Monlon and I saw your boyfriend Alex lying on the ground dead. He was holding an unfinished love note to you, telling you that he was dying.
I’m so sorry,
Herc N. Crashton
