Butterflies Bloom

A song for Quintus and Lleullin by Shade

Butterflies bloom in my garden
Fish fly free through my walls
I get drunk
drinking sunlight
Roses make me tall
(repeat)

In the Summer I go fishing
I catch blueberries with gold.
When the snow falls I go swimming
So I dont get cold.
