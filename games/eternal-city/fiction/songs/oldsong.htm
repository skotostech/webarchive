Maybe this Old Song

By Seraph

(A romantic love song)

Verse 1

Candles burning, Honey Mead is waiting and soon,
My husband will be by,
Hope and pray he will say that our Love,
Never dies!
And if my best laid plans they fail
To melt my Husband's heart.
I will sing this song of Love to him!

Chorus

And maybe this Old Song of Love,
Will bring back old times,
Making old lines sound new.
And he will lay his, head on my shoulder,
And his Midlight love for me will show through.
And maybe we will start to cry,
And wonder why,
Ereal gave us our love and showed the way.
And maybe this Old Song,
It brings back old times,
And our Love is here to Stay!

Verse 2

It has been so long since I,
Seen his face light up,
When he comes home.
It has been many hours I have wasted staring,
At the floor.
Sweet Old Song, I am counting on you,
To bring his love to me.
I am tired of crying alone.
