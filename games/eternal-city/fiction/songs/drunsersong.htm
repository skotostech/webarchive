A Good Ole Drinking Song

By Drunser

We're all sitting here,
(chorus)
Yes, we are! Nursing our beers,
(chorus)
Yes,we are! Sharing good songs,
(chorus; you get the point)
Flapping our tongues,
(chorus)
Chewing the fat,
(chorus)
Petting the cat,
(chorus)
Having great fun,
(chorus)
And drinking until... up comes the sun! Thank you very much.
