The Rites of The Path

By Malezzerai

Rites of the Path

Once ye walked with me .. in sundrenched fields and forests grey,
Now ye must walk alone... as i know that i must as well some day ...
I grieve my loss , my fallen kin.. sing sifaro and sifara all
With eyes wide open you walk the paths .. to the Incarna where you will stay.

Fear not the paths, with your heart so true.. so bright a life ye led
your pain is gone and your race is won .. would that i lay there in your stead.
Incarna call my kin to home.. And guide them along the Way
Kin of mine hear my call .. i will greet ye glad one day.

Road rise up to Meet them .... Erai'Theran ..oh lord light their way
Wind Blow at my kins Back ....this in song i pray.
I grieve my loss , my fallen kin.. sing sifaro and sifara all
With eyes wide open you walk the paths .. to the Incarna where you will stay.

your pain is gone and your race is won .. would that i lay there in your stead.

Commited to flame this Hollow shell ...To free my soaring Kin
Free from Pain and free from Strife .. never to again know Sin
I grieve my loss , my fallen kin.. sing sifaro and sifara all
With eyes wide open you walk the paths .. Incarna welcome them in.


~Malezzerai DaSallant~
~:-Esh' Erai'Theran-:~
