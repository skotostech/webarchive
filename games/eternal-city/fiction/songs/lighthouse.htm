Let the Lighthouse Light be Burning

By Seraph

(A Seaman's Hymn of Ereal, A song of the Iridinian Sea)

Verse 1

Out on the deep,
And stormy sea,
Some poor sailors are Tempest tossed and more.
But there is a light,
Shining from Iridine's harbor,
From the Lighthouse on the shore.

Chorus

Oh let the Lighthouse Light be Burning,
Sent from Ereal, across the waves.
Because some poor fainting,
Struggling seaman,
Will be rescued, and be Saved!

Verse 2

When on life's seas,
You are drifting.
And your soul is in a storm.
Look to the Light of Ereal,
That is in His Harbor,
From His Lighthouse on His Glorious Shore!

Chorus

Oh let the Lighthouse Light be Burning,
Sent from Ereal, across the waves.
Because some poor fainting,
Struggling seaman,
Will be rescued, and be Saved!
