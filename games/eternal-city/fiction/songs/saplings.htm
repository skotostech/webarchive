The whys of Saplings
an essay in verse
by Shade

To find a sapling,
seek a tree
Saplings only grow
where trees be.
Saplings, they make
bandit spears
and bandit spears
cause fightersí tears
Saplings also
fangstaves make
fangstaves, bandit bones
do break
To my mind saplings
serve us best
when pushing them
in quicksand tests
