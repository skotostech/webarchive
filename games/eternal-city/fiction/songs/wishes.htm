Wishes

By Shade

Sung at the Wedding of Elrina and Riticus

May the Sun always light your way before
And the darkness follow it behind
May the Moons lend you grace
While the Stars grant you peace
These are the wishes of my heart.

May Fire and Water always be your friends
May Earth and Air keep you from harm
May you always know kindness and always be loved
These are the wishes of my heart.

May you grow in Wisdom,
May you walk without fear
May you never know hunger or pain
May the Sun warm your souls,
while your friends warm your hearts
These are the wishes of my heart.

So may the Sun always light your way before
May the darkness cover it behind
May the Moons lend you grace,
while the Stars grant you peace
These are the wishes of my heart.
