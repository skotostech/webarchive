Cinerio Hope

When I was a baby the Cineran Legions wanted to take me away and train me to be a warrior. My parents refused and the senator gave them one week to change their mind. Instead of changing their minds my parents took me and ran. Arrows came out of the night and slayed my father. My mother tucked me in my blanket and gave me to a beggar just before an arrow struck her. I lived with that begger for a while but she soon had to give me up. I was given to a local orfanage where I was raised and lived until I was eighteen and left. So begins my life in Iridine...
