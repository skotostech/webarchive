Keylo Girfne

Keylo Girfne was born on the 1st day of Ereal in the 199th Year of the Republic and was an only child.  He was born in The Steps of the City of Iridine.  His name was the same as his father's (As was the tradition in his family.  The exact meaning and origin of the name is unknown due to the length of his family line and bad memory which runs in the family).  At the age of 8, Keylo was kidnapped by some bandits and taken to the kingdom of Cinera and was put into slave labor.  At 16 he managed to escape from his master and has returned to Iridine to start a new life...
