Olaric Adler


Being born and raised as a simple boy in a tough town, Olaric was tough to the blows of reality. When his parents moved to Iridine after their uncle had died of illness, they thought it would be the best for him. Olaric, learning the simple actions for his quarterstave, spent most of his time on the streets, looking for adventure. While still young, Olaric found himself drawn into Leda's where he trained hard everyday with his quarterstave. As he grew older he became stronger and better with his weapon. He had been living his his parents until they died of old age when he was just 19. He set out to Leda's to continue his training. Soon he found Leda's a comforting place for him. Olaric then taught himself how to fight in the sewers and his life began...
