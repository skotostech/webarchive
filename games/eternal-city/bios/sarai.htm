Sarai Agape



I was born to a happy, hard working family. My parents worked hard to make sure I was trained in what I love to do, Healing. Life was good for me. I had the perfect family, the perfect life, and the man of my dreams. His name was Abramus. I loved him so that I wanted to spend every waking hour with him. To my regret I often did. On one of those days while I was with him at his home my family was attacked in their home by a band of bandits. My twin sister, Exitt was able to fight them off with a kitchen knife, but not before my parents where both critically wounded. I returned home that night to find both my parents dead and my sister raving mad. From then on I vowed to dedicate myself to the gift my parents gave me that I should have been home to use. The gift of healing. And so began my journey to Iridine to advance my skill and save others in hopes of forgiveness for failing to save my parents....
