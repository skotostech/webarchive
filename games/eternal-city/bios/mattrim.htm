Mattrim

He was the son of a rich political figure in Altene. Mattrim hated his father. To the Altene people he was good. But everyone who knew him knew that Ron Cauthon was a terrible person to live with. When Mattrim had drunk too much in the tavern where he always came, people heard him yelling out stuff like 'HE'S THE BIGGEST HIPPOCRITE EVER, MY FATHER' and 'SOME DAY I'M GOING TO KILL HIM.' And then one day, the body of Ron Cauthon was found in an alley. Murdered. Knife trough the heart. So called 'witnesses' said Mattrim did it. The young man knew that he didn't, even though he had been drinking alot the night before and didn't remember anything of what happened. Even if it was a terrible person, it was still his father. And the witnesses? Bought, Mattrim thought. But who? And why? There wasn't much time to think about it. While the guards came to his room to arrest him, he jumped trough the window and ran, ran as fast as he could. He had always been a good runner, so he could easily get rid of the pesky guards chasing him. But he kept on running, untill he was tired. By then, he realized that he didn't know where he was. He was in a dark forest, overgrown by huge trees. He continued walking on for days. Hunting and fishing and avoiding dangerous animals to survive. After a long time of travelling, he arrived at an inn, called the Blue Breakwater Inn. That's where he heard how close he actually was to Iridine. Iridine, the eternal city. He couldn't believe he had travelled so far. He thought for a while, and then decided to go to Iridine and try to make a living there. When he arrived in Iridine, he got somewhat lost in the chaos. It had been almost a year, since the death of his father, that he had been in a city. After wandering around for a while, he ended up by an inn, the Stone Toga Inn. And old man stood there, observing him. "Hello there youngster," he said. "Hello," said Mattrim, "I'm new here, can you help me?" And as a response to that question the old man started to talk about life in Iridine. After the conversation, Mattrim entered the inn, ordered a cup of tea and sat down on a chair, thinkng about the fact that he couldn't live forever on 20 denars....
