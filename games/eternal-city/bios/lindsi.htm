Linds Freeman

Almost as soon as I settled down, I met a wonderful guy, Antequides, whom I am going to marry soon. When I first came, I just walked around a lot, but now I have learned how to make money and have fun. I like to help people out and despite what some people think of me, I manage to ignore them.
