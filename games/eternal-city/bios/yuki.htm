Yuki



She was born in the famed city of Iridine.
She lives in a shack on the Steps.
She has always been bookish, and kept to herself.
She had a great childhood, but was always treated unkind by others.
This has made her try to be the perkiest person you will know.
For those who know her say she is cute, entertaining, and fun to be around.
Close friend of Makina, Wolke, Rainee, Stryfe, and many others.
She is Always willing to lend a hand if able.
