Bobith Ni


Ever since a child Bobith has hated hoods, since a Cinera hood killed his father. Bobith and Capawinius swore vengence against the Cineras. Bobith is a skilled altene swordsman, seldomly backing down from a good fight.

He spent many years traveling and learning before coming upon the great city of Iridine, were after several months he was reunited with his brother.

He spends most of his time on adventures or finding other things to kill. With his thin retalq gladius and shield he is a worthy adversary.
