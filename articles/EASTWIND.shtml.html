About this series: “The Book of the East Wind” is one of the classics of Chan-la literature, dealing with history and prophecy, the past and the future. The Book of the East Wind article series, named after this classic book, deals with the past and the future of the Qigung game, as designed by its author and creator Jeff Crook.
