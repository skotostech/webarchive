About this series:

This series examines design considerations from live action role playing games, and considers what techniques might be transferable to online prose games from their more physical cousins.

Heather Logas has spent many happy years pouring her soul into online, table top, and live action role playing games. She has recently recieved her Master's Degree with a focus on game design and theory. This has instilled in her the belief that she now has the right to pontificate at will and make up words whimifically.

Josh Powell has worked in the game industry for 5 years at video game publisher Electronic Arts. He has a passion for online games and social networking sites and whimifically enjoys board games.
