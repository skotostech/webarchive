About this series: Storms on Cloud Nine is a column about the construction of online games with a focus on mechanics. The author, Scott Holliday, is currently working on the upcoming Skotos World "Orphan Crown". He likes games such as paper-rock-scissors and can sometimes win at tic-tac-toe.
