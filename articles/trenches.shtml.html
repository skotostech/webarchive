About this series: In The Trenches is a column offering insight and advice regarding actual StoryBuilding. The author, Laurel Stuart, is a new External Developer for Skotos Tech. She's been active as a game designer and staff member for numerous online prose games, most notably White Wolf's flagship online game, New Bremen.
