About this series:

Lessons from the Live Team looks at online game development from a post-launch perspective.

Sandra "srand" Powers served for several years in many positions - including producer, lead engineer, and game systems designer - on the live team of Asheron's Call 1, a major commercial MMORPG.

Now a producer with EverQuest II, she seeks to use her unique experiences for good, improving the virtual worlds that she so admires.
