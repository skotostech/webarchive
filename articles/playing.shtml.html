About this series: History is not always kind to a game designer. Playing with History is a column that examines the challenges associated with working in this genre. The author, Michael Karlin, is the project developer for the upcoming Skotos-Seven Theatre, Glory of the Nile. He likes long walks in the forest, travelling to exotic locales, and fighting crime.
